What is Mirco-Qubic?

Mirco-Qubic is a pay-per-use platform on the Qubic ecosystem. Instead of subscriptions, merchants turn digital services—APIs, tools, content—into billable resources with a fixed micro-price in QUBIC. Users pay only for what they use; payments are settled instantly with low-cost micropayments. You get one session per use, pay once, and the merchant grants access when payment is confirmed.
